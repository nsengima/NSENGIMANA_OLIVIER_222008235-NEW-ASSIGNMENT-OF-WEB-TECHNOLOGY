    • WELCOME TO TAILOR MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (TMS)

    A Tailor Shop Management System is a software application designed to streamline and organize various aspects of running a tailor shop or clothing store.

    Customer Management: TMS allows tailors to maintain a database of their customers, including their contact information, measurements, preferences, and order history. This helps in providing personalized service to customers and facilitates easy communication. Appointment Scheduling: Tailors can schedule appointments with customers for measurements, fittings, and consultations. The system can send automated reminders to both the tailor and the customer, reducing the chances of missed appointments. Order Management: TMS tracks all orders placed by customers, including details such as garment type, fabric choice, measurements, and delivery dates. It helps in organizing orders, managing deadlines, and ensuring timely delivery. Inventory Management: For tailors who sell fabrics and accessories, the system can track inventory levels, generate purchase orders for restocking, and provide insights into popular items. This ensures that the tailor always has the necessary materials on hand to fulfill orders.
